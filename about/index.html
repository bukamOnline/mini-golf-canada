About Mini Golf Canada

Mini Golf Canada helps people compare mini golf and mini putt courses across Canada.

Mini Golf Canada is a static, fast-loading directory built to help families, travellers, groups, and local searchers compare mini golf options by province, city, photos, prices, ratings, and visitor signals.

The goal is useful planning content: not just a name and address, but context that helps someone decide where to play.
