Privacy Policy

Privacy information for Mini Golf Canada visitors.

Mini Golf Canada is designed as a static website. Basic hosting logs may be collected by the hosting provider. The site uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate visitor activity, such as page views, traffic sources, device/browser details, and general usage patterns. Google may use cookies or similar technologies according to its own policies.

Location search uses your browser's geolocation permission only when you choose the location button. Your location is used in your browser to sort nearby listings and is not needed to browse the site.
