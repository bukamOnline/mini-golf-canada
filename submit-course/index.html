Submit or Correct a Mini Golf Listing

Send a new mini golf course, mini putt venue, or listing correction to Mini Golf Canada.

Course owners, visitors, and local operators can send updates for Mini Golf Canada. Submissions are reviewed before publication so the directory stays useful, accurate, and focused on real mini golf and mini putt options.

Submit a courseCorrect a listing

What to include

  • Business name, city, province, and official website.
  • Current address, phone number, and booking or pricing page if available.
  • Whether the venue is indoor, outdoor, glow golf, party-friendly, accessible, or connected to food, arcade, go-karts, bowling, batting cages, or a driving range.
  • Official photos or a public gallery link when available.
  • For corrections, include the current Mini Golf Canada listing URL and the official source for the change.

General questions can also be sent to nocturnaldevs@gmail.com or (437) 366-2920.
