About The Bunker

Looking for mini golf in Kingston, Ontario? The Bunker gives players an easy-to-plan putting outing with the practical details visitors usually want before choosing a course. The venue is listed as Miniature golf course, Adventure sports center, Bowling alley, Children's party service, Indoor golf course, which helps visitors understand the kind of experience to expect before they go. It is located at Beside Dollarama on lower level, 1300 Bath Rd, Kingston, ON K7M 4X4. The Bunker Alex McCoy Plumbing and Heating proudly supports The Bunker – Mini-Putt, Bowling & Sports Simulators (formerly Putt N’ Blast). The Bunker features Kingston’s only 18-hole indoor dark light mini-putt, 4 Duckpin Bowling lanes, and 3 multi-sport simulators bays (Bay 1, Alex McCoy Plumbing and Heating Bay, and Cupido Bay). As a hub for affordable family entertainment, The Bunker will partner with BGC South East, Kingston Wrestling Club, and Jung Do Karate to offer sports programs to local children and youth.
