Guido thought, ‘One day I’m going to build a golf course where everyone’s equal’ and that is how Shooters became Winnipeg's first family golf centre. With the original 9-holes opening in September of 1998 & the following 9-holes opening in 2000, Shooters Family Golf Centre is now an ex ecutive 18-hole golf course, 100 stall driving range, miniture golf, golf simulator facility (in winter months) & an excellent restaurant that is open year round.
