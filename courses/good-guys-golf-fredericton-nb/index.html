About Good Guys Golf

Good Guys Golf is an indoor mini putt venue in Fredericton, New Brunswick. Official sources list a 9-hole mini putt course, golf simulator, bar service, arcade and tabletop games at 100 Woodside Lane. Venue pricing lists mini putt at $6.99 for 9 holes, $4.99 for children under 13, and $3.99 for replay, with simulator pricing listed separately. Confirm current hours, prices and private-event availability with the venue before visiting.

Prices and planning notes

Mini putt pricing listed by the venue: $6.99 for 9 holes, $4.99 for children under 13, and $3.99 for replay. Golf simulator pricing is listed separately at $40 per hour for up to 4 players.
