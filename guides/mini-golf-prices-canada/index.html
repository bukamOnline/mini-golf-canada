Mini Golf Prices in Canada

A planning guide for mini golf admission, family passes, group rates, and add-on entertainment costs.

Prices vary by venue and season

Mini golf prices in Canada often depend on age, day of week, indoor versus outdoor setting, season, and whether the course is bundled with arcade credits or other attractions. Public price pages can change, so the directory treats prices as planning notes and sends visitors to official sources for confirmation.

Common price factors

  • Adult, child, senior, and family admission.
  • Replay discounts or second-round pricing.
  • Birthday packages and private event rates.
  • Combo passes with go-karts, laser tag, arcade, bowling, or driving range activities.
  • Taxes, booking fees, and seasonal specials.

When price details were found, they appear on individual course pages with context.
