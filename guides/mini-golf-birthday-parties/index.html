Mini Golf Birthday Parties and Group Events

How to compare mini golf venues for birthdays, school trips, team events, and family gatherings.

Mini golf works for mixed ages

Mini golf is a strong birthday and group-event activity because it works for kids, adults, and mixed-skill groups. The best venue depends on the group size, food options, party rooms, supervision needs, and whether you want add-ons like arcade games, go-karts, batting cages, or laser tag.

Party planning checklist

  • Confirm guest capacity and deposit rules.
  • Ask whether food, cake, or outside snacks are allowed.
  • Check package length and included activities.
  • Review accessibility, parking, and washroom details.
  • Compare recent photos so expectations match the course.
