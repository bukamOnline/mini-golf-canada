Mini Putt vs Mini Golf in Canada

Mini putt, mini golf, putt putt, and glow golf often describe the same family-friendly putting activity.

Canadians use both terms

In many Canadian cities, people search for "mini putt" and "mini golf" interchangeably. Some venues use one phrase in their official name, while reviews and local searchers use another. That is why city pages on Mini Golf Canada include both terms where they help visitors find the right course.
